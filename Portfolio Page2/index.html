1.Bio

I was born in Orlando. When I was three years old,
I moved to Japan and lived there for 15 years. So, my first language
is Japanese. In Japan, we need to take exams to enroll in universities.
The exams are so severe that many people cannot pass them and those
who did not pass them need to decide whether they continue studying for
next year's exams or go to their second-choice universities. Having studied
for five years in Japanese middle and high schools for university entrance exams
and having close friends in Japan, deciding whether to come to America or stay
in Japan was a very serious decision for me, but I decided to come here because
my parents stay here for their work.

When I was in Japan, I took a piano lesson for a year, a kimono lesson for
a year, calligraphy lesson for two years, a swimming lesson for six years, and
tea ceremony lesson for ten years and went to cram school for six years
to pass entrance exams for middle school and universities.
